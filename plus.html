What is PLUS?

PLUS Tutors is an online platform that connects students with qualified tutors across a wide range of subjects and academic levels. Designed to provide personalized, one-on-one tutoring sessions, it caters to individual learning needs and helps students grasp complex concepts while improving their academic performance. The platform features experienced and vetted tutors, ensuring high-quality instruction tailored to various learning styles. With flexible scheduling options, students can easily book sessions at convenient times. PLUS Tutors utilizes digital tools such as video calls and interactive whiteboards to create an engaging learning environment, emphasizing not only academic improvement but also the development of confidence and study skills. Overall, PLUS Tutors offers a comprehensive solution aimed at enhancing student learning and success.

My Role at PLUS<>

As a tutor at PLUS, I immersed myself in the handbook, which equips educators with strategies for engaging children and fostering enthusiasm for mathematics. My focus was on making calculus problems fun and relatable. For instance, when teaching division and fractions, I used the example of sharing a pizza, where the number of pizzas represented the numerator and the slices were the denominator, helping students understand fractions in a tangible way. Additionally, I shared simple tricks for converting decimals to fractions, such as counting the digits after the decimal point, removing the decimal, placing a 1 in the denominator, and adding zeros accordingly. This approach not only made learning enjoyable but also helped build their mental math skills.
