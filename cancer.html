Modeling and Simulating Cancer Dynamics Using the Moran Model

Authors :

John Cost, Pratham Lotia, Leila Michal, Akshra Paimagam

Abstract :

This initiative was dedicated to the final project for the course "Computational Methods for Biological Modelling and Simulation". Cancer progression is influenced by genetic mutations, selective pressures, and environmental factors. This project explores the stochastic Moran model to simulate cancer evolution, focusing on stage-specific probabilities, mutation dynamics, and immune responses. The study demonstrates how cancer cells proliferate under varying conditions and emphasizes early therapeutic interventions to control tumor growth.
